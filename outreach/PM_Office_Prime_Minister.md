### Right Honourable Prime Minister,

The **Public Enterprises Governance Amendment Bill (2025)** positions the Office of the Prime Minister as the single oversight authority for all State-Owned Enterprises. This is a historic opportunity — and it demands **standardized, digital tools** to monitor 81+ entities from day one.

**Hoop Africa Foundation** has spent the past two years conducting **doctoral-level primary research** into the strategic management processes of Namibian commercial SOEs. We interviewed 40 executives, managers, and board members across multiple entities. We attended the **JSA/JICA-sponsored M&E workshop** where monitoring and evaluation was flagged as a **national-level gap across the entire public sector**.

Having identified the bottlenecks — fragmented reporting, absent KPIs, superficial board evaluation, fiscal risk blind spots, and chronic non-compliance with annual reporting — **we built the solution**.

### What PEPAS Delivers

| Module | What It Solves |
|--------|---------------|
| **Performance Contracts & KPI Library** | Standardized governance agreements for all SOEs — the Act requires them, but none exist in practice |
| **Quarterly Scorecard Portal** | Replaces Excel/Gantt chart tracking with structured digital reporting, evidence uploads, and corrective action workflows |
| **SOE Fiscal Risk Dashboard** | Treasury-level visibility into liquidity, arrears, guarantees, and subsidy dependency across the entire portfolio |
| **Compliance Tracker** | Monitors annual report submissions — currently only 5 of 12 major SOEs publish on time |
| **Board & Executive Evaluation** | Outcome-based evaluation replacing attendance-based peer reviews |

### Why Now

- **Budget pressure**: SOE subsidies cut from N$1.3B to N$615.7M — entities must demonstrate performance or face consequences
- **Ghana's SIGA model**: The Namibian delegation (Sept 2024) saw how performance contracts scaled from 6 to 79 entities in 8 years — PEPAS is the digital backbone to replicate this
- **Centralisation window**: As OPM takes oversight, deploying PEPAS ensures the office has monitoring capability from the start
