### Dear CEO,

Epangelo Mining received **zero subsidy allocation** in the FY 2026/27 Budget. Government is signalling that entities managing strategic mineral assets must demonstrate commercial viability and measurable performance. Centralised oversight under the Office of the Prime Minister will bring standardised reporting requirements.

Hoop Africa Foundation's two-year research programme across Namibian commercial SOEs identified that **monitoring and evaluation is a national-level gap** — confirmed by the JSA/JICA-sponsored M&E workshop. We built **PEPAS** as the digital platform to close this gap.

### What PEPAS Delivers for Epangelo Mining

| Feature | Value to Epangelo |
|---------|------------------|
| **Fiscal Health Dashboard** | Track operating margins, cash flow, capital expenditure, and revenue from mineral assets in real-time |
| **Performance Scorecards** | Standardised quarterly KPI reporting aligned to NDP6 and ministerial expectations |
| **Governance Agreements** | Digital performance contracts compliant with the PE Governance Act |
| **Corrective Action Tracking** | Formalised escalation for underperformance — accountability documented and auditable |
| **Portfolio Benchmarking** | Epangelo's performance scored against the full national SOE portfolio |
