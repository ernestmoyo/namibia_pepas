### Dear CEO,

TransNamib is at a critical inflection point. With **zero subsidy allocation in FY 2026/27**, government is signalling that sustainable performance is no longer optional. At the same time, centralised oversight under the Office of the Prime Minister will bring increased scrutiny on how entities manage strategy, report performance, and demonstrate accountability.

Hoop Africa Foundation has conducted **in-depth primary research** within TransNamib and other commercial SOEs. We spoke with your executives and managers about the realities of strategic management — from strategy formulation to implementation to evaluation. We understand the challenges:

- Performance management systems that are **partial or union-contested**
- Policies and procedures that are **20+ years old and largely obsolete**
- An evaluation framework that is **not in place below executive level**
- Reliance on **Excel and Gantt charts** for strategy tracking

We did not conduct this research to write another report. **We built a tool to help.**

### What PEPAS Delivers for TransNamib

| Feature | Value to TransNamib |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Structured Quarterly Reporting** | Replace Excel/Gantt tracking with digital submissions, evidence uploads, and variance explanations |
| **Corrective Action Tracking** | Every underperformance triggers a documented corrective action with owner, deadline, and escalation |
| **KPI Standardisation** | Aligned to Balanced Scorecard, NDP6, and ministerial expectations — no more ad-hoc metrics |
| **Fiscal Health Monitoring** | Track liquidity, arrears, and operating margins in real-time — critical during the zero-subsidy period |
| **Board & Executive Evaluation** | Outcome-based assessment that goes beyond attendance records — measuring real delivery |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS drills down to TransNamib specifically. The demo currently shows TransNamib's performance score, risk classification, and trend data alongside the full national portfolio. With your collaboration, we integrate real operational data for a production-ready turnaround monitoring tool.
