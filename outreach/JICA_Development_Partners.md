### Dear Development Partners,

The **JSA/JICA-sponsored Monitoring and Evaluation workshop**, convened through the Ministry of Public Enterprises, identified a critical national gap: **monitoring and evaluation is not taking place across Namibia's public sector**, including its 81+ State-Owned Enterprises.

This finding was confirmed through our own **two-year primary research programme** — 40 in-depth interviews with executives, managers, audit, HR, legal, and operations teams across multiple Namibian commercial SOEs. The PhD-level research (University of Johannesburg) documented:

- No standardised M&E framework exists despite legislative requirements
- SOEs track strategy with Excel spreadsheets and Gantt charts
- Board evaluations are reduced to attendance-based peer reviews
- Consequences for non-performance are absent
- Policies and procedures are 20+ years old in some entities

**Having identified the gap together, Hoop Africa Foundation built the solution: PEPAS.**

### What PEPAS Is

PEPAS (Public Enterprises Performance & Accountability System) is a **working digital platform** — not a concept note or framework document — that operationalises SOE monitoring and evaluation:

| Module | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Performance Contracts & KPI Library** | Standardised governance agreements with comparable KPIs across all SOEs |
| **Quarterly Scorecard Portal** | Digital reporting replacing ad-hoc submissions — evidence uploads, corrective actions, board packs |
| **Fiscal Risk Dashboard** | Treasury-level portfolio view of liquidity, arrears, guarantees, and subsidy dependency |
| **Compliance Tracker** | Annual report pipeline, automated deadlines, public disclosure hub |
| **Board & Executive Evaluation** | Outcome-based evaluation across 5 dimensions — strategy, finance, governance, risk, development |

### Alignment with JICA's Namibia Programme

PEPAS directly supports JICA's focus areas:
- **Accelerating Economic Development** — SOEs control 40% of GDP in assets; improving their performance has macro-economic impact
- **Capacity Building** — PEPAS provides institutional infrastructure, not just training
- **Governance Strengthening** — Aligns with the Public Enterprises Governance Act and the incoming centralised oversight framework
