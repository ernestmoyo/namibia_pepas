### Dear Managing Director,

Namibia Wildlife Resorts manages some of the country's most iconic tourism assets. Tourism is a strategic pillar of Namibia's economy, and as government centralises SOE oversight, NWR's ability to demonstrate measurable performance — occupancy, revenue generation, service delivery, conservation outcomes — becomes essential.

Hoop Africa Foundation conducted extensive research into Namibian commercial SOEs and found a sector-wide gap in monitoring and evaluation. **PEPAS** is the digital platform we built to address this.

### What PEPAS Delivers for NWR

| Feature | Value to NWR |
|---------|-------------|
| **Performance Dashboard** | Entity-level KPIs tailored to tourism — occupancy, revenue per available room, customer satisfaction |
| **Fiscal Health Monitoring** | Track operating margins, seasonal cash flow, and infrastructure maintenance spend |
| **Quarterly Reporting Portal** | Structured submissions replacing ad-hoc reporting — evidence uploads and corrective actions |
| **Portfolio Benchmarking** | NWR's performance scored against the full national SOE portfolio |
| **Board Evaluation** | Outcome-based assessment aligned to tourism service delivery and conservation mandates |
