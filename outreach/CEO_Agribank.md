### Dear CEO,

Agribank received **zero subsidy allocation** in the FY 2026/27 Budget — a direct signal from government that self-sustainability is the expectation. In this environment, the ability to demonstrate measurable performance and fiscal health to oversight bodies is not optional — it is survival.

Hoop Africa Foundation spent two years conducting **primary research into Namibian commercial SOEs**, interviewing 40 executives and managers. We found a sector-wide absence of standardised monitoring and evaluation tools. We built **PEPAS** as the national solution.

### What PEPAS Delivers for Agribank

| Feature | Value to Agribank |
|---------|------------------|
| **Fiscal Health Dashboard** | Track liquidity, loan portfolio quality, operating margins, and capital adequacy in real-time |
| **Performance Scorecards** | Standardised quarterly KPI reporting aligned to Balanced Scorecard and NDP6 |
| **Corrective Action Tracking** | Formalise response to underperformance with documented escalation and deadlines |
| **Compliance Monitoring** | Annual report deadlines, audit outcomes, and statutory filing tracked automatically |
| **Portfolio Benchmarking** | Agribank's performance scored and compared against peer financial services SOEs |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS includes entity-level drill-down for Agribank — performance gauges, risk classification, fiscal indicators, and trend data. The demo runs on simulated data. With your engagement, we integrate real Agribank data for a production-ready monitoring tool.
