### Dear Managing Director,

NamPower is one of Namibia's most strategically significant SOEs — responsible for national energy security and a major contributor to fiscal stability. As government moves toward **centralised SOE oversight under the Office of the Prime Minister**, the expectations on entities to demonstrate measurable performance will intensify.

Hoop Africa Foundation has conducted **extensive primary research** into how Namibian commercial SOEs manage strategy development, implementation, and evaluation. Across 40 interviews with executives and managers, we found a consistent gap: **the absence of standardised, digital monitoring and evaluation tools**.

We built **PEPAS** to fill that gap — and it works at the **entity level**, not just the portfolio level.

### What PEPAS Means for NamPower

| Feature | Value to NamPower |
|---------|------------------|
| **Performance Dashboard** | A single view of NamPower's KPIs, score trends, and quarterly performance — ready for board reporting |
| **Governance Agreement Portal** | Digital performance contracts aligned to NDP6 and ministerial expectations — version-controlled and auditable |
| **Quarterly Reporting** | Replace ad-hoc reporting with structured submissions, evidence uploads, and corrective action tracking |
| **Fiscal Health Indicators** | Liquidity, arrears, debt service coverage, and operating margins — benchmarked against the portfolio |
| **Board Evaluation** | Outcome-based evaluation across strategy delivery, financial stewardship, governance, risk management, and development outcomes |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS drills down to each SOE individually. Your board and executive team can see **NamPower's performance score, risk tier, compliance status, and trend data** — all in one place, comparable to peers across the portfolio.

The platform currently runs on **demonstration data** for 12 major SOEs including NamPower. With your participation, we can integrate real operational data to deliver a live, production-ready monitoring tool.
