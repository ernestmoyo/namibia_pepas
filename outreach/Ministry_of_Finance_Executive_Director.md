### Dear Executive Director,

Namibia's SOE portfolio controls assets worth **~N$120 billion (~40% of GDP)** with government guarantees of **N$7.6 billion**. In the 2026/27 Budget, subsidies were cut from N$1.3B to N$615.7M — a clear signal that fiscal discipline is the priority.

**The Ministry needs real-time fiscal risk visibility across the portfolio.** Currently, there is no standardized tool to aggregate liquidity positions, arrears, debt service coverage, and guarantee exposure across 81+ entities into a single dashboard.

Hoop Africa Foundation conducted **two years of primary research** — 40 in-depth interviews across commercial SOEs — and identified that fiscal risk monitoring is fragmented, annual reports are chronically late, and there is no mechanism for early warning signals.

**We built the solution: PEPAS.**

### What PEPAS Delivers for Treasury

| Capability | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Fiscal Risk Dashboard** | Real-time view of liquidity runway, operating margins, arrears ratios, debt service coverage, and guarantee exposure per entity |
| **Risk Tiering** | Automatic classification of entities as Low / Medium / High / Critical risk — with early warning triggers |
| **Subsidy Dependency Tracking** | Monitor which entities remain dependent on government transfers and which are progressing toward self-sufficiency |
| **Compliance Pipeline** | Track annual report submissions against statutory deadlines — currently only 42% compliance |
| **Performance Scorecards** | Quarterly KPI data from every SOE, eliminating reliance on ad-hoc Excel reporting |

### The Fiscal Case

- **N$7.6B in government guarantees** require active monitoring — a single SOE default could trigger a material fiscal event
- **Clean audits dropped from 13 to 6** — the trend is worsening, not improving
- **TransNamib, Agribank, Epangelo** received zero allocation in FY 2026/27 — real-time tracking of their financial health is critical
- **Ghana's SIGA** achieved 127/175 entities filing annual reports (up from 16) — PEPAS enables the same compliance improvement
