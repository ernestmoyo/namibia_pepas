### Dear CEO,

MTC is consistently recognised as one of Namibia's **best-performing SOEs** — leading in financial returns, governance, and annual reporting. Our research identified MTC as a benchmark for what strong strategic management looks like in the Namibian context.

As the Government of the Republic of Namibia moves toward **centralised oversight** and standardised performance contracting for all SOEs, MTC's strong position can be **amplified and made visible** through PEPAS — the digital platform we built specifically for Namibia's SOE portfolio.

### Why PEPAS Benefits High Performers Like MTC

| Feature | Value to MTC |
|---------|-------------|
| **Performance Visibility** | MTC's strong scores are visible in the national portfolio dashboard — demonstrating value to government and the public |
| **Benchmarking** | Compare MTC's KPIs against the full portfolio — proving performance leadership with data, not anecdotes |
| **Streamlined Reporting** | Replace manual quarterly and annual reporting workflows with digital submissions and evidence uploads |
| **Board Pack Generation** | Automated, standardised board packs drawn from live KPI data |
| **Compliance Showcase** | MTC's annual report compliance and clean audits are tracked and displayed — reinforcing the gold standard |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS includes **entity-level drill-down** for each SOE. MTC's dashboard shows performance gauges, risk tier, compliance status, and trend data — all benchmarked against peers. The platform currently uses demonstration data for 12 SOEs. With MTC's participation, we integrate real data to create a production-ready tool.

### Live Demo

We welcome the opportunity to present a tailored demonstration to your executive team.
