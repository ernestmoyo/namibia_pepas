### Dear Managing Director,

NamibRe plays a vital role in Namibia's financial services ecosystem, providing reinsurance capacity that supports national insurance markets. As the Government moves toward centralised SOE oversight, every entity — including strong financial performers — will be required to report through standardised frameworks.

Hoop Africa Foundation's two-year research programme identified a national gap in monitoring and evaluation across Namibian SOEs. **PEPAS** is the digital platform we built to close that gap.

### What PEPAS Delivers for NamibRe

| Feature | Value to NamibRe |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Performance Dashboard** | Entity-level KPIs, score trends, and quarterly performance at a glance |
| **Fiscal Health Indicators** | Operating margins, investment returns, solvency ratios benchmarked against financial services peers |
| **Standardised Reporting** | Digital quarterly submissions aligned to incoming OPM oversight requirements |
| **Compliance Tracking** | Annual report and audit outcomes monitored against statutory deadlines |
| **Portfolio Benchmarking** | NamibRe's performance scored alongside peer financial services SOEs |
