### Dear CEO,

Telecom Namibia operates a Balanced Scorecard cascaded through a three-trimester system with quarterly dashboard reporting — one of the stronger internal performance management practices among Namibian SOEs. Our research confirmed this during interviews with your executives.

However, our research also identified areas that the new centralised oversight framework will scrutinise:

- **Board evaluation remains superficial** — "They do this peer review. Who attended how many meetings, what is their contribution in discussions. There's nothing tangible we can give on the table."
- **Standardised government reporting** requirements are incoming under the Public Enterprises Governance Amendment Bill

PEPAS is designed to **build on what works** and close the remaining gaps.

### What PEPAS Delivers for Telecom Namibia

| Feature | Value to Telecom Namibia |
|---------|------------------------|
| **Standardised Reporting Portal** | Align existing trimester reviews with the incoming national quarterly reporting standard |
| **Board & Executive Evaluation** | Move from attendance-based peer review to outcome-based evaluation across 5 dimensions |
| **National Benchmarking** | See Telecom Namibia's performance scored and ranked alongside the full SOE portfolio |
| **Corrective Action Tracking** | Formalise follow-up on underperformance with documented escalation workflows |
| **Compliance Dashboard** | Ensure annual report deadlines are met and visible to oversight bodies |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS includes a dedicated entity view for Telecom Namibia — performance gauges, risk tier, compliance status, and trend data. The platform runs on demonstration data today. With your engagement, we integrate real operational data for production use.
