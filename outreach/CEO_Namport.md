### Dear CEO,

Namport is a cornerstone of Namibia's trade infrastructure — managing the Port of Walvis Bay, one of Southern Africa's key logistics gateways. As government centralises SOE oversight, entities managing strategic national assets will be expected to demonstrate measurable performance through standardised reporting.

Hoop Africa Foundation has spent two years researching how Namibian commercial SOEs manage strategy and performance. Our findings — from 40 interviews across multiple entities — revealed a consistent national gap in monitoring and evaluation. We built **PEPAS** to close that gap.

### What PEPAS Delivers for Namport

| Feature | Value to Namport |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Performance Dashboard** | Entity-level view of Namport's KPIs, quarterly trends, and overall performance score |
| **Fiscal Health Indicators** | Operating margins, revenue trends, debt service coverage, and capital expenditure tracking |
| **Governance Agreement Portal** | Digital performance contracts aligned to NDP6 and incoming OPM oversight requirements |
| **Quarterly Reporting** | Structured submissions with evidence uploads — ready for centralised government reporting |
| **Portfolio Benchmarking** | Namport's performance benchmarked against peer transport and logistics SOEs |

### Your Entity, Your Data

PEPAS drills down to Namport specifically — showing performance gauges, risk tier, compliance status, and trend data within the national portfolio. Currently running on demonstration data. With your participation, we integrate real Namport data for a production-ready tool.
